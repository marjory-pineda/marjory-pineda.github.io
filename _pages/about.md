## Biography

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Human-Centered Computing (HCC) Program at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). My advisor is <a href="https://ykotturi.github.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dr. Yasmine Kotturi</a>. I received my M.S. in Human-Centered Computing in 2024 and my B.A. in Business Technology Administration in 2022, both from UMBC. 

My research interests include:

- Community-engaged work 
- Human-centered AI
- The future of work
- Accessibility

I am particularly interested in investigating the dual role of technology in marginalized communities: in what ways does technology serve and empower and/or fail and exclude marginalized communities?
